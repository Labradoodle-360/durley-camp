Who We Are

Durley Camp is a small Free Methodist Youth Camp that is located a few miles outside of Greenville, IL.

It has been hosting hundreds of campers of all ages, for many generations. The campgrounds are also available for special events.

Our Mission

"Our mission is to assist the local church in making disciples through camping initiatives where youth and adults can experience the life transforming message of Jesus Christ and are challenged to grow deeper in faith."
